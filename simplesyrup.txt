Simple Syrup

Simple Syrup is a fundamental cocktail ingredient, used for adding sweetness to
a drink without altering its flavor or having to deal with solids.

1 part white sugar
1 part water
1 medium saucepan
1 dispensing container

Mix sugar and water in medium saucepan. Boil water, ensuring all sugar is
dissolved. Remove from heat, and let cool. Once cool enough to not damage
dispensing container, transfer to dispensing container and refrigerate.

Don't keep for more than a few days to a week.

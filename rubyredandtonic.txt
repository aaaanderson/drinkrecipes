Ruby Red & Tonic

1 part Ruby Red grapefruit vodka, chilled
1 part tonic water or soda water, chilled
(optional) 1 dash grapefruit bitters
(if ingredients unchilled) ice
cocktail glass, or other wide-mouthed glass

If ingredients chilled:

Add vodka to glass. Add tonic water to glass. If using grapefruit bitters, add
to glass. Mix lightly and serve.

If ingredients unchilled:

Fill a separate cup with ice. Add vodka to cup. Add tonic or soda water to cup.
If using grapefruit bitters, add to cup. Mix well. Strain into glass.

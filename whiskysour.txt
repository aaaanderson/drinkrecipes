Whisky Sour

2 parts whisky (can be shitty)
1 part simple syrup
1 part lime juice
ice
low-walled glass, or if necessary a solo cup

See simplesyrup.txt for simple syrup recipe.

Fill glass with ice. Add whisky, simple syrup, and lime juice. Mix well.

May be served with a straw.

Substitute lemon juice for lime juice if desired.
